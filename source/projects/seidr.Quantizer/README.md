## Quantizing Modes
0. The whole keyboard.
1. Traditional scale.

## Rounding Modes
0. UP
1. DOWN
2. UP_DOWN
3. DOWN_UP
4. UP_OVERFLOW
5. DOWN_UNDERFLOW
6. NEAREST
7. FURTHEST
